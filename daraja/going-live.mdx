Wondering: **Should I choose a Paybill or a Till (Buy Goods)?**

### Paybill vs Till: What to Choose

Your choice depends on how you plan to use M-Pesa:

- **Only collecting online payments via STK push?** → Use a **Till**
- **Need to track offline transactions or require unique account references?** → Use a **Paybill**

<Tip>
  We recommend applying for a **Paybill** for long-term flexibility. It supports everything a Till does, plus more.
</Tip>

## Requirements

Prepare these documents:

- [Paybill Application Form](https://assets.mpesaflow.com/daraja/paybill-form.pdf)
- [Tariff Guide](https://assets.mpesaflow.com/daraja/tariff-guide.pdf)
- [Account Opening Form](https://assets.mpesaflow.com/daraja/account-opening-form.pdf)
- [Admin Account Creation Form](https://assets.mpesaflow.com/daraja/admin-account-creation-form.pdf)
- [Administrator Letter (Sample)](https://assets.mpesaflow.com/daraja/administrator-letter-sample.pdf)
- Bank letter or canceled cheque
- KRA PIN Certificate
- Copy of ID/Passport

**Stamp all documents.** Sign or stamp your bank letter.

<Note>
  Personal Till and Paybill accounts cannot be used for M-Pesa API integrations.
</Note>

## How to Apply

Choose one of these methods:

### Option 1: Business Portal (Recommended)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the M-PESA Business Portal">
    Go to [MPESA Business Portal](https://m-pesaforbusiness.co.ke/).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Account or Log In">
    Create an account or log in with existing credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Required Documents">
    Upload all documents in PDF format. Ensure you've stamped and signed everything properly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and Wait">
    Submit your application. Safaricom processes applications within **72 hours**.
    No response in 24 hours? Check your documents and reapply.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Option 2: Email Application

<Steps>
  <Step title="Compile Documents">
    Scan all required documents into a **single PDF** file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Email">
    Email: [M-PESABusiness@safaricom.co.ke](mailto:M-PESABusiness@safaricom.co.ke)\
    CC: [paybill@safaricom.co.ke](mailto:paybill@safaricom.co.ke)\
    Subject: **Paybill or Till Application**\
    Include your contact information in the email body.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach and Send">
    Attach your PDF document and send. Double-check that all documents are complete and properly filled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Response">
    Expect a response within **72 hours** if all documents are correct.
    No response in 24 hours? Verify your application and resend.
  </Step>
</Steps>
